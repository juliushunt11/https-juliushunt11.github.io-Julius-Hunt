By Julius Hunt

Action Games

I love action games that are fast paced with some story telling. Some of my favorites are *DOOM Eternal*, *Battlefield* and *Red Dead Redemption 2*.

Horror Games

I love playing horror games like *Resident Evil* and *Dead by Daylight*.

Racing Games

I love playing racing games like *Need for Speed* and *Forza* that let's me modify and race different types of cars.

RPGs

I love story-driven RPGs like *Dark Souls* and *Elden Ring* where I can explore the map and level up my character.
